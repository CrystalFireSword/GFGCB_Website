# **App Name**: CampusCoders Hub

## Core Features:

- Navbar Navigation: Sticky navbar with links to Home, About Us, Team, Advent Calendar, and Contact Us pages for easy navigation.
- Home Page Showcase: Hero section with GFG Campus Body title, one-line description, and GFG-themed visuals to quickly establish identity and purpose.
- Team Structure Display: Display of organizer and teams sections with placeholder data for name, year, designation and profile picture. Teams included are Design, Editorial, Events, Technical and Social Media.
- Advent Calendar Interface: A 24-day grid representing the Advent Calendar. Clickable tiles reveal day number, DSA concept title, and a short explanation, without backend logic.
- Contact Form Submission: Contact form with fields for name, email, and message, plus optional fields for college name.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color (light mode): GeeksForGeeks Green (#00895e) to align with brand guidelines.
- Primary color (dark mode): GfG Ocean (#3ab284).
- Secondary color (light mode): GfG Navy (#105d95).
- Secondary color (dark mode): GfG Blue (#016ccc).
- Other Colors: #f1f8f5, #d5f6e4, #3ab284, #3ab284, #265645, #0e201b, #ecfcfc, #bfe0f8, #016ccc, #105d95, #0b3760, #002b46
- Accent Colors: #C3F96E, #FF9843, #FCFF78, #7D94F9, #0A0E0F, #E9E9EA, #F5F5F7
- Primary Font: 'Sofia Pro'.
- Secondary Font: 'Source Sans 3'.
- Tertiary Font: 'Nunito'.
- Component-based structure for maintainability and scalability.
- Mobile-first responsive design to ensure accessibility across devices.

- Use the logo wherever applicable
